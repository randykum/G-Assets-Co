Business Name: G’Asset Co.
Business Model: Hire-purchase (Buy Now, Pay in Installments)
Location: Cameroon (Douala for now) (specific office 'siege social' not determined yet) no office at the moment
Sector: Asset Financing / Micro-Enterprise Support
This business provides income-generating assets to individuals and small business owners who cannot afford upfront purchase. Customers use the asset immediately while making scheduled payments. Ownership is transferred only upon full payment.


Mission
To empower financially constrained individuals by providing essential business assets through flexible hire-purchase agreements, enabling them to generate sustainable income.
Vision
To become the leading micro-asset financing company in Cameroon, promoting job creation and economic growth through responsible lending practices.
Core Values
•	Transparency
•	Social impact
•	Financial empowerment
•	Integrity
•	Innovation
