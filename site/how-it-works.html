FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to apply?

You'll need a valid national ID card, proof of residence (utility bill or rental agreement), and a clear business plan or intended use for the asset. No formal credit history required.

How long does approval take?

We aim to review all applications within 24 hours. In most cases, you'll receive a decision on the same day you apply. Once approved, delivery can happen within 2-3 days.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Orange Money), bank transfers, and cash payments. You can choose the method that's most convenient for you.

What if I miss a payment?

Life happens, and we understand. Contact us immediately if you're having difficulty. We work with our customers to find solutions, including payment rescheduling when needed.

Is there a warranty on the equipment?

Yes! All our equipment comes with a warranty. If there's a defect or issue during the warranty period, we'll repair or replace it at no extra cost to you.

Can I pay off early?

Absolutely! We encourage early payoff. There are no penalties for paying ahead of schedule. In fact, you may qualify for a discount if you complete payments early.
